# README: 1.newick.tre

## Title
Seed plant phylogeny for reef fish analysis, derived from Dryad dataset doi:10.5061/dryad.fc71cp4

## Source Publication & Dataset
- **Dataset**: Dryad Digital Repository. 10.5061/dryad.fc71cp4  
- **Original study**: Smith, S.A. & Brown, J.W. (2018). Constructing a broadly inclusive seed plant phylogeny. *Nature* 554: 518–522.

## Date of Access & Processing
- Original dataset accessed: July 9, 2025  
- File generated: July 9, 2025, by trimming the tree to include reef-fish species used in our study.

## File Format
- File: 1.newick.tre  
- Format: Newick (UTF-8 encoded, single-line tree)  
- Contains: tip names (OTUs) matching species in combined_results_2024-09-17.csv

## Provenance and Modifications
- Source tree imported from Dryad dataset doi:10.5061/dryad.fc71cp4  
- Pruned to include only species present in our empirical synthesis  
- No modification to branch lengths, node labels, or topology  
- Rooting status preserved from the original dataset

## Usage in Current Study
- Incorporated into 3_phylogenetic_analysis.R  
- Used to calculate phylogenetic signal and incorporate evolutionary history in modeling β (density dependence)
