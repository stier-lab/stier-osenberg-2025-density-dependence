# README: actinopt_12k_treePL.tre

## Title
Time-calibrated phylogeny of Actinopterygii (ray-finned fishes) derived from Smith & Brown (2018) dataset (Dryad doi:10.5061/dryad.fc71cp4)

## Source Publication & Dataset
- **Dataset**: Dryad Digital Repository. doi:10.5061/dryad.fc71cp4  
- **Original study**: Smith, S.A. & Brown, J.W. (2018). Constructing a broadly inclusive seed plant phylogeny. *Nature*, 554: 518–522.

> Note: Although originally part of a broader phylogenetic compilation, this tree (`actinopt_12k_treePL.tre`) specifically reflects the Actinopterygii subtree from that repository.

## Date of Access & Processing
- Original Dryad dataset accessed: July 9, 2025  
- File processed to extract relevant Actinopterygii subtree used in reef fish analysis

## File Format
- File: `actinopt_12k_treePL.tre`  
- Format: Newick (UTF-8 encoded)  
- Type: Chronogram (ultrametric, time-scaled)  
- Branch lengths: millions of years before present (based on treePL calibration)

## Provenance and Modifications
- Extracted from the Smith & Brown (2018) Dryad dataset (`phylogeny.tre` with treePL timing)  
- Pruned and formatted to retain only ray-finned fish clades relevant to our synthesis of reef fish density dependence  
- Rooting and topology preserved from original file  
- Tip names may have been matched or aligned with reef fish species in `combined_results_2024-09-17.csv`

## Usage in Current Study
- Used in `3_phylogenetic_analysis.R` for phylogenetic signal and meta-analytic modeling  
- Supports time-aware modeling of trait evolution in reef fish density dependence (β estimates)

## Related Files
- Final parameter estimates: `combined_results_2024-09-17.csv`  
- Phylogenetic modeling code: `3_phylogenetic_analysis.R`
